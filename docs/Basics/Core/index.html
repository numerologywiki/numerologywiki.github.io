Core

The “core” refers to the five main numbers that form the foundation of your chart and are considered the most important influences in your life.[1][3][7]

The five core numbers​

  • Life Path number – from your birth date; shows your life’s overall purpose and the “path” you’re on.[3][7]
  • Expression (or Destiny) number – from your full birth name; reveals your talents, potential, and how you naturally express yourself.[7][3]
  • Soul Urge (Heart’s Desire) number – from the vowels in your name; reflects your inner desires and what truly motivates you.[3][7]
  • Personality number – from the consonants in your name; describes how others first perceive you.[7][3]
  • Birthday number (or Birth Day number) – the day you were born; highlights specific gifts and tendencies you carry.[1][3]

Together, these core numbers are treated as the “building blocks” of your numerology profile and are used first when interpreting a chart.[4][1][7]
